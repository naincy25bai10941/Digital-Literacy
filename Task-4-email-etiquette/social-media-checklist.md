## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
### Do’s:
* Think before posting anything online, as it can stay
there permanently
* Keep your profile simple and professional
* Use strong and unique passwords
* Respect others while interacting online
* Verify information before sharing it
### Don’ts:
* Don’t share personal information like phone
number or passwords
* Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content
* Don’t believe or forward unverified messages
* Don’t click on suspicious links
* Don’t waste too much time on social media
