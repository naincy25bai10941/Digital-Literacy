## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
* Try not to share your OTP, PIN or bank
details with anyone, even if the person
sounds genuine
* Avoid clicking on random links or
messages that look suspicious or too good
to be true
* Always double-check before making any
payment or approving a request on UPI
apps
* Don’t scan unknown QR codes, especially
if someone asks you to “receive” money
through it
* Use strong passwords and don’t keep the
same password for every account
* Turn on two-factor authentication
wherever possible for extra safety
* While using public Wi-Fi, avoid logging
into important accounts like banking apps
* If something feels unusual, it’s better to
stop and verify first instead of acting
quickly
## For UPI / Payment Safety
* Remember that you don’t need to enter
your PIN to receive money
* Never approve a payment request unless
you are sure who send it
## In case of fraud
* Report immediately on: cybercrime.gov.in
* Call helpline: 1930
